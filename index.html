The Tasmanian Tiger

The Tasmanian tiger(Thylacine) is a carnivorous marsupial from the island of Tasmania that was hunted untill extinction because they were scene as a nuisance for eating the locals live stock.

The Great auk

The Great auk is a flightless bird that lived near the sea in the reagions off of the North Atlantic coasts, they became extinct from sailors using the birds as a source of food and became endangered. Museums and private collectors wanted to taxidermy the animal so they could remember what it looked like, they put bounty on the already threatened species head which only accelerated their extinction.
